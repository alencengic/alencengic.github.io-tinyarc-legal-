Frequently asked questions

How do I log my day?

Tap Start My Day on the Home screen (or open Daily Entry from the menu) and follow the five quick steps: mood, meals, sleep, water, and productivity.

Where do I see my insights?

Open Trends & Analytics, Mood Analytics, or Weather & Mood from the Home screen or side menu. Weekly insights appear on the Home screen under "Your Insights".

How does Apple Health sync work?

On the sleep step of your daily entry, tap Sync from Apple Health to fill in last night's sleep automatically. You can manage Health access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data to export your mood, food, weather, and journal data as a PDF report.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Account → Change Password.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. After you confirm, you are signed out and your account is scheduled for deletion: your account and all of your data (moods, meals, journal entries, sleep, and everything else) are permanently deleted after 30 days. If you change your mind, simply sign in again within 30 days to cancel the deletion.

How do I manage ad privacy?

If you're in a region with consent requirements (EEA/UK), you can change or withdraw your ad consent anytime under Settings → Ad Privacy → Manage Ad Consent.
